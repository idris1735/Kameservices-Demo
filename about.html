À PROPOS DE NOUS

KAME Logistics a été créé pour fournir des services intégrés d'importation et d'exportation maritimes aux entreprises du monde entier à partir de la Côte d'Ivoire, notre pays d'origine.

En tant que fournisseur de services de classe mondiale moderne et soutenu par les TIC, nous sommes spécialisés dans les services de transport et de logistique multimodaux, et nous sommes réputés pour fournir à notre clientèle des solutions sur mesure qui répondent carrément à leurs défis logistiques dans quatre domaines principaux, à savoir : Transit, Import-Export et conseil.

Équipe

Notre équipe de travail acharné

EVELYNE KANGA

Managing Director

Kanga is a young lady with an overwhelming passion for the import-export transit, logistics, and international trade with over a decade of experience in the field. She took her first steps at Diamond Shipping in 2013 as a Sales Representative. She then climbed the ladder through hard work and landed the position of Director of Operations at PERFORM WORLD where she was responsible for managing agricultural export operations, a position she brilliantly held for 3 years. Rich in experience and personal skills, Ms. Kanga decided in 2019 to actualize her dream, that of revitalizing the transit and logistics sector by starting her first company KAME SERVICES PLUS specialized in import-export transit by sea, land, air, and supply chain management. Ms Kanga is now positioned as an expert and wears a second hat, that of import-export consultant since 2019 at GIE GEPPA - 1st world exporter of cashew nuts.

FRANK KOUDIO

Chief Operating Officer

Mr. Frank Kouadio has more than thirteen years of experience in the management of logistics transport operations. After his professional degree in logistics, he successively held the positions of Fleet Manager and Logistics Manager within SOFT DRINKS and SOCOTRA, and managed operations in a multinational cashew exporter. He joined KAME SERVICES as Chief Operating Officer with the mission of project and human resources management in order to contribute significantly to the success of the company's projects.

Bruno M'Bouké

Operations and Handling Manager

Mr. M'Bouké holds a professional degree in Marketing Management. He has to his credit, more than 9 years of experience in the maritime and trading field. After a rich and varied professional career with companies such as Deloitte, ADM and Olam, Mr. M'bouké has decided to put his know-how and expertise at the service of KAME SERVICE PLUS by occupying the position of Operations and Handling Manager. He is working hard to expand his department.
